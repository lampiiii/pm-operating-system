# Products

Container for product-specific workspaces.

## Current Products

- `customer-success-management/` — Example product workspace demonstrating the full structure. Has its own `CLAUDE.md` with full structure, workflows, and conventions. Use this as a template for your own products.

## Conventions

- Each product gets its own top-level subdirectory with a dedicated `CLAUDE.md`
- Product directories follow the standard structure: `strategy/`, `features/`, `data-analysis/`, `research/`, `documentation/`
- When adding a new product, create the directory and a `CLAUDE.md` scoped to that product
